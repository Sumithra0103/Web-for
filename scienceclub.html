We Foster Creativity & Innovation

Science Club has grown into a vibrant community of over 50-60 members who share a passion for creativity and technology. Our mission is to provide a space where ideas can flourish and collaborations can happen naturally.

We believe that the best innovations happen when diverse minds come together. That's why we welcome individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels.

Events Conducted

28 Aug 21 Oct

The first edition of iCUBE

A state-level technical event among the engineering colleges in Tamilnadu, held between 28th August 2021 and 21st October 2021. The event witnessed participation from the top colleges across the state where students were encouraged to came up with innovative ideas pertaining to the theme "Sustain to Remain". Under the event, "Innowel", participants have come up with a lot of innovative approaches for the given problem statements across various categories, and their ideas were assessed based on novelty, feasibility, etc. On the other hand, teams that participated in the event "Idea-o-lite", identified existing problems and crafted new and efficient solutions. Students underwent a rigorous evaluation process by both internal members include and external members include ( Mahesh Shankar, CTO, Influx Worldwide, San Francisco, California , Kothand Rajangam,Founder/ CEO @ Katpro Technologies,Florida, USA, S. Ganapathy Gangadharan, Chief Innovation Evangelist, Tata Consultancy Services, Florida, USA).The team from SVCE placed third in the event, "Innowel". Under the event "Idea-o-lite", the team from SVCE emerged as winners. Dr. S. Ganesh Vaidyanathan, Principal, SVCE welcomed all the invitees and conveyed his wishes to the winners. He mainly briefed about the SVCE Science Club, the importance of events like iCUBE. Dr. M. Sivanandham, Secretary - Sri Venkateswara Educational & Health Trust, congratulated all the winners and participants of the event.

25 Jan

Sparring for Science

Sparring for Science is a virtual debate/group discussion like event held on 25th January 2022 in Google meet between 2:15pm and 5:30pm. It is similar to the Model United Nations in which the teams can discuss major environmental, technological, ecological topics across the globe. The event started with welcome note by the Coordinator SVCE Science Club Dr.N.R.Sheela and special address by Dean Research Professor R.Muthucumaraswamy.
